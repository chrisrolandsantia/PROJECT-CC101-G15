Cookies Policy

GameZone is a retro-themed student project and does not use cookies of any kind. No tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or functional cookies are implemented, and no data is stored on visitor devices through cookie technology. All features of the website operate without requiring cookie-based personalization or user identification. If future updates introduce tools or features that require cookies, clear information will be provided and this policy will be updated accordingly. At its current stage, GameZone functions entirely without cookies and remains focused on demonstrating web development skills for educational purposes. Thank you for visiting GameZone!
