Game Summary

Resident Evil 2 is a remake of the 1998 survival horror classic. Instead of simply updating the original, this reimagining rebuilds the game with redesigned environments, updated character models, and modern gameplay elements. It features over-the-shoulder combat similar to Resident Evil 7: Biohazard while maintaining the intense atmosphere and puzzle-focused progression of the original.

Story

The story follows rookie police officer Leon Kennedy and college student Claire Redfield as they arrive in Raccoon City during a catastrophic zombie outbreak. Each character has their own playable campaign, showing the events from different perspectives. Together, they must uncover the dark truth behind the outbreak, survive overwhelming horrors, and escape the crumbling city before it’s too late.
