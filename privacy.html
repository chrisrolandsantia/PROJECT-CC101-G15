Privacy Policy

GameZone is a retro-themed student project created for educational purposes and is designed with privacy in mind. The website does not collect, store, or share personal information, as it operates without accounts, forms, cookies, or tracking tools. All game titles, images, and references are included solely for demonstration and remain the property of their respective owners. The project focuses on showcasing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills, and all displayed information is provided without the use of analytics or user-data processing. If future updates introduce features involving data handling, this policy will be updated transparently and clearly. GameZone currently functions with no data collection of any kind and serves only as a learning and presentation project. Thank you for visiting GameZone!
