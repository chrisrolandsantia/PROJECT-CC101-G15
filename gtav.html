Game Summary

Grand Theft Auto V is set in the vibrant city of Los Santos, following three main characters whose lives intertwine through chaos, crime, and survival. You can switch between them as the story unfolds.

Story

Los Santos is a massive city filled with stars, self-help gurus, and people trying to survive an economy on the edge.

Three criminals — Franklin, Michael, and Trevor — team up for a series of high-risk heists as they chase money, freedom, and redemption.
